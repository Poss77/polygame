    About PolyGame

    PolyGame is a Web3 arcade ecosystem built on the Polygon network. It features a decentralized, serverless architecture powered by Supabase. Players can earn PGT (PolyGame Token) through gameplay, purchase utility NFTs to boost their earnings, and stake their tokens to earn high-yield APY.

    All player progression is automatically synced. Connect a Web3 wallet (like MetaMask) to secure your progress on-chain, climb the real-time leaderboards, and invite friends via our 4-tier referral system to earn passive commissions.
